
		New Zhengzhou Museum to open on April 30
	<p align="center">
	<a href="/n3/2021/0428/c90000-9844612-2.html"><img alt="" src="http://www.people.com.cn/mediafile/pic/BIG/20210428/90/9851315440128927134.jpg" /></a>
	<em>A painted clay figure of Buddha's head is displayed at the new Zhengzhou Museum in Henan Province, April 27, 2021. (Photo/China News Service)</em>
	More than 60,000 cultural relics were moved to the new museum.
	Covering a area of 147,000 square meters, the 62-meter-high new museum will officially open on April 30.
